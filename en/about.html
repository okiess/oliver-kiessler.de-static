About me

Fullstack Software Developer & IT Consultant (Freelance)
Expert in TypeScript / Javascript, Node.js, Angular and AWS

With over 25 years of experience as a software developer and IT consultant, I support businesses in developing custom software solutions – either independently or as part of an existing team. I work primarily remotely, ensuring clear communication and efficient collaboration throughout the project.

What You Can Expect

  • Clear Communication: I work proactively, clarify uncertainties early, and keep you consistently informed about progress, open topics, and next steps.
  • Transparency: You have full visibility into code, progress, and workload at all times.
  • Technical Depth: I bring deep expertise in architecture, backend, frontend, and DevOps – approaching your requirements holistically.
  • Reliability: I deliver with full commitment and high quality standards – from concept to production.

I can help you develop software, digital products, and tools – or streamline your internal processes with tailored software solutions. I think entrepreneurially, contribute ideas when wanted, and always keep your goals, budget, and requirements clearly in focus.

What matters to me is a clean, maintainable, and understandable codebase following Clean Code principles. I work with structured Git workflows and rely on continuous integration and automated testing to ensure software quality and stability from the start.
